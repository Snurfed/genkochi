Privacy Policy

Genkochi

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Genkochi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Photos: When you take or select photos for language learning, these images are processed to identify objects and generate vocabulary. Photos are sent to our AI service (OpenAI) for analysis but are not stored permanently on our servers.

Audio Recordings: When you use speech practice features, your voice is temporarily processed for pronunciation feedback. Recordings are not stored after processing.

Location Data: If you grant permission, we collect approximate location to place vocabulary on your learning map. This data is stored locally on your device.

Learning Progress: Your vocabulary, quiz scores, and learning statistics are stored locally on your device to track your progress.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide language learning features and personalized content
  • To generate vocabulary from your photos using AI
  • To provide pronunciation feedback through speech recognition
  • To track and display your learning progress

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For photo analysis and vocabulary generation. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Speech Recognition: For pronunciation practice (processed on-device when possible).

Data Storage

Most of your data (vocabulary, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain user accounts or store personal data on external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with service providers (like OpenAI) as necessary to provide app functionality.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your local data by uninstalling the app
  • Disable location permissions in your device settings
  • Disable microphone permissions to opt out of speech features

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
